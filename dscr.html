The Concept

DSCR in one minute

DSCR = monthly rent ÷ monthly payment (principal, interest, taxes, insurance). A ratio at or above 1.0 means the property pays for itself — and that's what we lend on.

WHO IT'S FOR

Buy-and-hold & BRRRR investors

Building a rental portfolio, refinancing a finished flip into a keeper, or scaling past what banks allow on personal income — DSCR is built for it. Self-employed and full-time investors welcome.

WHAT WE LOOK AT

The rent, the property, the market

Actual lease or market rent, property condition and taxes, insurance, and local rental demand. Your personal DTI never enters the equation.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

A short, sane document list

Lease agreement (or market rent support), entity documents if applicable, and insurance. That's the shape of it — no shoebox of tax returns.

THE PAIRING

Flip → rental, one lender

Finish a flip on our bridge financing and roll straight into a DSCR loan to hold it — one relationship covering the whole BRRRR cycle.

Under the Hood

How the financing works — three borrower paths

We broker across a network of private capital sources, so one application reaches multiple programs. Which path you land on is mostly about credit and experience:

Strong credit (700+)

The sharpest pricing — rates from around 10%, typically 20% down, closings as fast as 24 hours once the file is complete.

Flexible credit

Lower or thin credit with flip experience: higher rate, down payment 10–20% depending on profile. The deal still gets done.

Asset-based — no credit required

Underwritten 100% on the deal itself. If the numbers work, no credit score is needed. Priced for the risk, built for speed.

Every program: 6-month terms built around your exit, interest-only monthly payments, rehab funds held in escrow and released on draw inspections, LLC-friendly, non-owner-occupied only.

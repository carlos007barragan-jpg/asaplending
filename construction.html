The Process

How draw funding works

Simple discipline that protects both sides of the deal.

01

Budget & schedule set at closing

Your construction budget is broken into phases with a draw amount attached to each — everyone knows what releases when, before the first hammer swings.

02

Complete a phase, request a draw

Finish framing, rough-in, drywall — whatever the phase is — and request the draw for it.

03

Quick inspection, fast release

A site check verifies the work, funds release, crews keep moving. No month-long bank committee between you and your own budget.

04

Finish, then exit

Sell the finished build, or refinance into a DSCR rental loan and keep it producing.

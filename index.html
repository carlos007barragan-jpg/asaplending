Program 01 · Fix & Flip

How a flip loan actually works

Asset-based bridge capital — acquisition plus renovation, funded on draws, underwritten on the deal instead of your tax returns.

01 · THE DEAL

We underwrite the asset

Three numbers drive everything: purchase price, rehab budget, and ARV — set by what comparable renovated homes actually sold for near your property.

02 · THE SCOPE

Your scope of work sets your ARV

A clear line-item renovation plan lets us comp your property against homes rehabbed to the same standard. Better scope, better valuation, more leverage.

03 · THE DRAWS

Rehab funded in stages

Finish a phase, a quick inspection verifies it, the next draw releases. Budget stays covered, crews stay moving, nobody fronts the whole rehab.

04 · THE EXIT

Sell — or hold it

Interest-only during the project. Exit by selling the finished flip, or roll into a DSCR rental loan with us and keep it.

You'll need: purchase contract · scope of work · your ARV estimate (we verify free) · entity docs & insurance. No W-2s, no tax returns.  Submit your deal →

Program 02 · DSCR Rental

The property qualifies. Not your W-2.

DSCR = monthly rent ÷ monthly payment (principal, interest, taxes, insurance). At or above 1.0, the property pays for itself — and that's what we lend on. No income verification.

Who it's for

Buy-and-hold and BRRRR investors, portfolio builders, self-employed and full-time investors banks turn away. Lend directly to your LLC.

The pairing

Finish a flip on our bridge capital, refinance into a DSCR loan to hold it — one lender covering the whole cycle. Docs: lease or market-rent support, entity docs, insurance.

Program 03 · Construction & Heavy Rehab

Draws that keep crews moving

Ground-up builds and major renovations, funded in stages against completed work — budget phased at closing, quick inspection per phase, fast release. The project never waits on money.

Questions

Straight answers

What kinds of financing do you offer?

Multiple models: asset-based fix & flip capital, DSCR rental loans, construction and rehab financing with draws, and custom structures for deals that don't fit a box. One conversation covers all of them.

Why don't you publish rates?

Because we'd be lying to somebody. Terms depend on the deal — asset, market, budget, exit, experience. Submit yours and you'll get real numbers built for it, usually the same day.

Where do you lend?

Missouri, Kansas, Florida, Texas, Arkansas, and Nebraska — headquartered in Kansas City, where we buy and renovate property ourselves.

Do you check my credit or income?

The deal carries the weight. Asset-based means we're underwriting the property and your plan — self-employed and full-time investors welcome.

How fast can you close?

Days on clean deals, not weeks. No bank underwriting queue — bring title-ready and we move at investor speed.

Do you fund wholesale double closes?

Yes — transactional funding for A-to-B closings paid off by your end buyer the same day. Bring both contracts and clean title.

Straight answers

What do you lend on?

Kansas City metro fix-and-flip and value-add residential. We underwrite the asset: purchase price, rehab budget, and ARV backed by real comps.

How fast can you actually close?

Days, not weeks. Asset-based means no bank underwriting queue — bring a clean deal with title ready and we move at wholesale speed.

Do you check my credit or income?

The deal carries the weight. We're lending on the property and your plan for it — self-employed and full-time investors welcome.

Do you offer rental financing?

Yes — DSCR loans for buy-and-hold investors, qualified on the property's rent coverage rather than personal income, plus other financing programs case by case.

Who's behind ASAP Lending?

The Real Living family of companies — a Kansas City operator that buys, renovates, sells, and manages property across the metro from a real office at 7017 Oak Street. We lend where we live.
